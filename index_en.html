Workshop on Clouds and Applications - XIV

In conjuction with SBRC 2016

Welcome

The WCGA series of workshops purpose is to act as a forum for technical presentations of relevant ongoing research activities in the areas of infrastructure and virtualized infrastructure, services and applications in different areas, bringing together researchers and professionals actively acting in this areas. The workshop also tries to establish multi-institutional collaborative networks and technical and scientific competence groups, as well as to strengthen ongoing activities. The workshop has been evolving to theme of Clouds since 2010, always approaching large scale computational environments. In 2016, WCGA will reach its 14th edition, get consolidated as one of the main workshops in the area of computer networks and distributed systems. Best paper of the workshop should be invited to a journal special issue.

Topics

Para a edição deste ano, autores são particularmente encorajados a submeter trabalhos originais em tópicos relacionados a Clouds (Nuvens) e Aplicações. Os tópicos de interesse incluem (mas não estão limitados a):

  • Virtualized Infrastructure
  • IaaS, PaaS, SaaS, NaaS, XaaS
  • Public, Private, Community and Hybrids Clouds
  • New Architectures and Models
  • Green Clouds
  • Support to Scalable and Elastic Services
  • Performance Evaluation
  • Fault Tolerance
  • QoS Provisioning
  • APIs and Usage Control
  • Clouds Composition
  • Resource Management and Scheduling
  • New Programming Models
  • Management Policies
  • Fog Computing
  • Mobile Cloud Computing
  • Management of Virtualized Infrastructure
  • Security in the Virtualized Infrastructure
  • Management of DataCenters
  • Applications and Services in the Virtualized Infrastructure
  • Deployment, Maintenance and Management of Services and Resources
  • New Applications
  • Scientific Applications
  • Support to Mobile Applications
  • Big Data on Clouds
  • Applications, Communities and Testbeds
  • Case Studies on Cloudses
  • Integração Cloud-IoT

Submission

Paper submission is electronic only. Authors should use the SBC submission URL. Papers may be in Portuguese or English, exclusively in PDF or (generic) Postscript. The paper should have no more than 14 pages, including abstract, figures, diagrams, references and appendices. Paper format should follow the SBC format.

Papers submitted to WCGA will be peer-reviewed, with accepted papers to be presented and published in the workshop proceedings. At least one of the authors should register for the SBRC and WCGA, and present the paper at the workshop.
